GETTING HERE

From the parking structure A, head east on Downey Way. At your first opportunity, turn left onto a pedestrian walkway. Walk up the path until you see the Brain and Creativity Institute on your left. There will be a shiny metal post out in front. When you get to the door, ring the doorbell and someone will buzz you into the building. In the lobby, you’ll check in with the receptionist and she’ll call Sarah. Should you get lost, please feel free to call Sarah or Priscilla at (213) 740-09282 (office) or (323) 557-3782 (cell).

For Families

Lyfts can be ordered whenever you are ready to go -- just let us know where and when!

For Kids Traveling Alone

If you wish to send your child our lab without you, we have a ride service specially designated for that, called HopSkipDrive. This is a service that uses drivers with childcare experience and extensive background checks. If you would like this option for your child, we must be notified a day in advance.
