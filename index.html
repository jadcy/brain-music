MUSIC, ARTS, AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT PROJECT

The goal of the MUSIC, ARTS and CHILD DEVELOPMENT research project is to investigate the impact of arts and music training on children’s cognitive, emotional, social, and brain development.

This research project is a collaborative effort between USC Brain and Creativity Institute, the Los Angeles Philharmonic YOLA program, and Camino Nuevo Charter Academy.
